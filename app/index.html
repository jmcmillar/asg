Who We Are

In 2002, ASG became the first independent life settlement portfolio management company, and has subsequently managed thousands of policies, with death benefits exceeding $5 billion. ASG has adopted a solutions-oriented approach in which we tailor our services to meet your mission critical needs and long-term objectives. In addition to the management of life settlement portfolios, ASG has also played an integral role in multiple legal cases including bankruptcies, receiverships and conservatorships, and recently expanded into the legal sector offering solutions related to bankruptcy noticing, claims, investor communications and back office support.
