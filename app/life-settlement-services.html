Premium Optimization
Identifying opportunities to minimize your premium outlay is key. We fully support a dual approach to premium management, utilizing both long-term and short-term optimized schedules.

Recognizing opportunities to minimize premium expense while maintaining the integrity of the asset is key. At ASG, we fully support a dual approach to premium management, utilizing both long-term and short-term optimized premium schedules. Long-term optimized premium schedules are vitally important, but short-term optimizations provide much more precise premium streams, often identifying opportunities for premium reduction beyond that outlined in the long-term stream, as well as any critical shortfalls that might be upcoming. Our team of policy specialists are experienced in effective premium management, and educated in the detail frequently hidden within life insurance contracts that can have a major impact on premium requirements.

Policy and Portfolio Management

Recognizing opportunities to minimize expenses while maintaining the integrity of the asset is key. Our team of portfolio managers have well over ten years of experience in life settlements, including:

  • Thoroughly reviewing each policy contract to ensure that all options and riders are recognized and considered, including critical information such as maturity dates, maturity extension riders, lapse/grace triggers and secondary guarantees.
  • Annual reevaluation of optimized premium schedules, which includes a review of projected rate increases, policy expense charges, premium load costs and No Lapse Guarantees, if applicable, to ensure that the optimized premium schedule is providing the client with the best possible cost savings.
  • Premium payment administration and confirmation.
  • Effectively managing carrier issues and communications.
  • Optimizing returns through effective death claims processing. Having processed nearly 1000 death claims, ASG’s experience will ensure that death certificates, and ultimately the claims proceeds, are received as quickly as possible.
