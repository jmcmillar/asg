Our Officers

Tom Moran

CEO

Mr. Moran has devoted his entire career to the insurance industry. In 1998, he directed his focus to the life settlement industry and established The Heritage Group Agency, Inc. a life settlement brokerage agency. Then in 2002, he opened the first independent life settlement servicing company in the industry. Mr. Moran also has extensive experience as a court appointed Receiver, Trustee and Conservator for several entities with settlement assets.

Mr. Moran has held positions on the Board of Directors of the Institutional Longevity Markets Association (ILMA), as well as The Oklahoma State Education Employees Group Insurance Board.

Sheri Townsend

COO

Ms. Townsend has worked within the life settlement industry since 1998 and co-founded ASG in 2002. She has been instrumental in managing the day-to-day operations of the company, while playing a key role in the successful takeover and management of several failed viatical and life settlement companies, some for which she is the Court Appointed Assistant Receiver or Conservator.

Ms. Townsend is a Member of the Board of Directors for the Life Insurance Settlement Association (LISA). She has been a licensed Life and Health agent for over 18 years. Ms. Townsend holds a degree in Business, with honors, from Belmont University in Nashville, Tennessee.
